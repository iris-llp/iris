Traditional Methods of Data Transmission in the context of AI

Traditional Methods of Data Transmission comprises of two ways. The first method is where AI features are transmitted from edge devices to the cloud for end-processing. The latter involves processing some fixed features at the edge and then sent to the cloud for storage. This means increased costs as organsiations processes a lot of data wishes to add more AI models.

IRIS Method

IRIS's proposed method involves installing low-cost embedded components at your edge device and software optimisation at your cloud server. This allows your organisation to benefit for a flexible form of data transmission, transferring computing load , reducing bandwidth , improved security and allow your organisation to process more for less !
